拾果 CMCA

Chinese Music and Culture Association

Mission

The Chinese Music and Culture Association fosters music, cultural enrichment, and artistic collaboration within the Chinese community across the United States. CMCA supports a wide range of cultural and artistic initiatives that serve musicians, dancers, visual artists, writers, and performers across disciplines.

Core Goals

  • Provide performance opportunities for artists and community members.
  • Promote cultural understanding and cross-cultural dialogue locally, nationally, and internationally.
  • Support artistic growth through education, mentorship, and community engagement.
  • Serve as a resource for artists seeking career development and collaboration opportunities.

Programs & Activities

CMCA organizes and supports performances, community events, workshops, masterclasses, and educational outreach. We partner with cultural institutions, educational organizations, and local businesses to expand our reach and impact.

Key Initiatives

  • Artist Residencies and Fellowships
  • Youth Arts Development
  • Community Arts Events and Festivals
  • Community Social Events and Cultural Celebrations
  • Workshops and Masterclasses
  • Creative Exchange Programs
  • Arts Group Incubation and Management
  • Fundraising for Charitable Causes

Current Groups

  • Find A Cappella — A semi‑professional, not‑for‑profit a cappella group with fixed members, rehearsing weekly and performing annually.
  • Sudo Chorus (also called Sudo Choir) — A not‑for‑profit choir that rehearses weekly and performs multiple concerts each year.
  • Drop‑In Chinese A Cappella — A public, informal community group that meets monthly to sing a cappella songs, open to all.

Impact Vision

Through its activities, CMCA aims to build an inclusive community that celebrates diversity in the arts and nurtures creativity. We aspire to leave a lasting impact on both the Chinese community and the wider arts landscape through collaboration, education, and cultural exchange.
