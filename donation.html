Support Chinese Music & Culture

Your gift sustains CMCA’s choirs, a cappella programs, youth arts, and community cultural events.

Ways to Give

  • Individual donations — Make a tax‑deductible contribution to CMCA. We will provide a receipt for your records.
  • Corporate matching — Many employers match charitable donations. We’re happy to complete any verification paperwork.
  • In‑kind support — Donate rehearsal space, printing, audio gear, refreshments, or professional services.
  • Volunteer — Help with events, production, education, outreach, or operations.

Get Started

Email us at operations.cmca@gmail.com to arrange your donation or ask questions. We will share current giving options and mailing details.

Your Impact

Gifts directly support rehearsals, concerts, workshops and masterclasses, youth arts development, and cross‑cultural exchange programs.

CMCA is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Donations are tax‑deductible to the extent allowed by law. This site does not sell or promote restricted goods or services.
