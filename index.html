Welcome to CMCA

Chinese Music and Culture Association (CMCA) is a nonprofit based in the San Francisco Bay Area. We foster music, cultural enrichment, and artistic collaboration for singers, musicians, dancers, visual artists, writers, and performers across disciplines.

Members come from all walks of life—many are tech professionals who unwind and connect through singing after work.

Learn more about us

Our Programs

  • Find A Cappella — Semi‑professional a cappella group performing annually.
  • Sudo Chorus (also known as Sudo Choir) — Weekly rehearsals; multiple concerts yearly.
  • Mandarin A Cappella Workshop — Monthly, drop‑in community singing open to all.

CMCA also supports youth arts development, artist residencies, workshops and masterclasses, community arts events, creative exchanges, and incubation for emerging arts groups.
