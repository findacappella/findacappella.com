Welcome to CMCA

We are a nonprofit music organization based in the San Francisco Bay Area, focused on promoting a cappella and choral music within the Chinese community and beyond. Our members come from all walks of life, especially tech professionals who unwind and connect through singing after work.

Learn more about us

Our Programs

  • Find A Cappella - A semi-professional, not-for-profit a cappella group performing annually.
  • Sudo Chorus - A choir that rehearses weekly and holds multiple concerts per year.
  • Mandarin A Cappella Workshop - A monthly public gathering for casual community singing.

We also aim to support new group creation and host broader music and arts events in the future.
